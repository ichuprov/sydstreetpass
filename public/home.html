Sunday 18th of March 2018 - 12am - 5pm

15 Broadway, Ultimo, New South Wales 2007

It's time for our first Nintendo Meet Up for the year!
Hope you can all come!
Bring your Switch and 3DS and games!
From Mario Kart to Bayonetta's Tag Climax to Kirby Star Allies!
Come join us!! And bring your friends too ;)

What is that?

Sydney Nintendo & StreetPass Fans is a community run group for people in NSW who enjoy Nintendo games.
Our focus is on members from NSW being able to get together for bi-monthly meetups which involve gaming and a lot of streetpassing!
You can also ask for gaming related help and we advocate a friendly environment.

What to Bring with ya?

3DS / 2DS

Come joining us and streetpass a maximum!
A lot of our members love playing Mario Kart, but also Pokemon, Zelda Triforce heroes, and to be honest, anything you would like.

Switch

If you are a lucky Switch owner, bring your console, your joycons, your games and enjoy a party on Splatoon, Arms, Snipperclips and co!

Any console!

We love Nintendo but we are firstly open-minded gamers.
Every platform has its gems, and if you want to share it with others, don't hesitate more!
Bring your PSVita, your Neo geo, Game Gear, Lynx, or even your smartphone!
